Glazed Carrots!

Description:

Carrots but glazed

Ingredients:

  • Carrots
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • Seasonings

Steps:

  • Cook the carrots: Place the peeled and cut carrots in a saucepan, cover with water, and bring to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer until the carrots are tender. Drain.
  • Glaze the carrotsMelt the butter in a saucepan, then add the sugar, salt, and pepper. Add carrots and toss until well-coated.
